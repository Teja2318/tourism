Top 10 Cultural & Tourism Places in India

Taj Mahal

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

The Taj Mahal, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Jaipur City Palace

Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan

The City Palace in Jaipur is a stunning blend of Rajput, Mughal, and European architecture, offering a glimpse into royal history.

Meenakshi Temple

Location: Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Meenakshi Temple is a historic Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar.

Qutub Minar

Location: Delhi

The Qutub Minar is a 73-meter-high tower built in 1193, a UNESCO World Heritage Site showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture.
